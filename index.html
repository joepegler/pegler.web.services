How I think

Opinionated on execution and reliability.

Why in-house bundler

Relying only on Zerodev or Pimlico means you inherit their latency, retry behaviour, and failure modes. We needed control over fallback, nonce handling, and simulation so we could tune for our traffic and debug when things broke.

Tradeoffs

Fallback and retries add latency. Fewer retries mean more failures. I design for observable tradeoffs: clear metrics, so the team can choose reliability vs speed and see the cost.

Debugging production

Simulation vs execution mismatches, nonce races, wallet contention, RPC lag (e.g. Base). I go from logs and metrics to root cause. Reproduce, fix, add guards so it does not recur.
